What Makes a Great Startup Name?

Before the list, a quick framework. The best startup names share five traits: they're easy to pronounce in any language, they're short (ideally under 3 syllables), they're distinctive enough to trademark, they work as a .com or strong alternative, and they're flexible enough to survive a pivot.

Use this list as a starting point — then validate before you commit.

How These Names Were Selected

  • Short (typically under 3 syllables) and easy to pronounce globally
  • Distinctive phonetics (more likely to be trademarkable)
  • Flexible enough to survive a pivot
  • Designed to work with .com or strong alternatives (.io / .ai)
