Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about startup trademark validation

Does a free domain mean the trademark is free?

No. Domain names (like .com or .io) are completely separate from legal trademarks. You can easily buy an available domain name and immediately infringe on a registered trademark.

Is this report legal advice?

No. NameProof provides a professional, AI-assisted risk assessment to help founders filter out bad names early. Before filing an official trademark, you should always consult an IP attorney.

Which databases do you check?

We check signals against the major global databases including USPTO (United States), EUIPO (European Union), and WIPO (Global intellectual property).

What if my chosen name is high risk?

If our assessment flags a high probability of trademark conflict, our report will also include 3-5 AI-generated alternative names that maintain your brand vibe but are safer to use.
